How to play?

Connect four of your checkers in a row while preventing your opponent from doing the same. Players take turns on placing their pieces. The pieces fall straight down, occupying the lowest available space within the column. The objective of the game is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own checkers.
